Understanding the CoolSculpting Process

The CoolSculpting treatment begins with the application of a specialized device that safely delivers cooling to targeted fat areas. Once frozen, the fat cells begin to break down and are eliminated through the body’s lymphatic system. This process continues over time, usually showing visible results within three weeks, with full results in approximately two to three months.

What to Expect: Before, During, and After

We make sure every client is fully prepared and informed before their treatment begins.

Before
